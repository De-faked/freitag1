ReDI School is a non-profit organization based in Germany that aims to provide digital
education and integration programs for refugees and other immigrants. Founded in 2016,
ReDI School offers courses invarious digital skills such as coding, web development,
and digital marketing.The organization seeks to empower its students by equipping
them with the necessary skills to integrate into the job market and society in their new home countries.







ReDI School operates on the belief that digital skills are crucial for successful integration in
today's society, where technology plays an increasingly important role in many aspects of life and
work. By offering these courses, ReDI School not only helps individuals enhance their
employability but also fosters diversity and inclusion within the tech industry.
